Hello Social Media

We’ve jumped on the social media bandwagon! Because every hip company’s gotta have some hip social media accounts, right? You can find us on Facebook, Twitter, YouTube and Instagram as @re-pranks. Side note: we sincerely promise we won’t spam your feed 24/7. Keep an eye out for those by following us on social media!

New Prank - Third Wheel

We’ve added a new prank - Third Wheel! This prank has the same central idea of the first two, Apocalypse and Crime Scene, with an added spice of awkwardness. Our co-owners actually came up with Third Wheel after a long and hilarious discussion at a local coffee shop about how they always end up third wheeling their friends. Funny story: Veronica always ends up sitting in the back of the car like a kid when she third wheels! She says it’s okay though, because she’s a kid at heart. We hope you like the new prank, and you’re always welcome to give us any suggestions by email or phone!

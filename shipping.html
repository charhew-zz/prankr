SHIPPING

Let's Talk "Shipping"

Well, we're not exactly shipping a physical product here, so the "shipping" cost is basically paying for our employees to travel to your target's location OR depending on how foreign the location is, hiring temporary employees. Fees range in price depending on the target's location and will be displayed accordingly during checkout. The reasons why the prices are different is because our employees mostly reside in British Columbia, Canada, so the cost of travel for them add up if they traveled far. This also accounts for the cost of temporarily hiring new employees from your target's region, to further create the reality of the prank.

The Prank

Step 1: We gather a crowd.

We grab around fifty of our employees, give them the what's-up, get them fired up and loud, and even dress them up in tattered clothing just to make the apocalypse extra convincing.

Step 2: We send them to your target's house.

We round everybody up, hop into our preferred mode of transportation (usually the Batmobile), and we get everyone over to your target's house!

Step 3: They scream, yell, and surround the house!

Our employees are running like they've never run before, screaming like they've never screamed before. It's an apocalypse, after all! They'll pound on the doors, shout at your target to let them in, and even try climbing in through the window. Your target is probably completely freaking out at this point, and it'll be as hilarious as you would imagine.

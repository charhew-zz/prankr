The Prank

An hour before your target is due to come home, we gather up tables, seats, and plenty of romantic candles and set them up on your target’s lawn. We’ll then fill up the tables with around 15 different couples, and they’ll be acting like they’re on a romantic date. Your target will come home to 15 couples on the lawn, and our videographers will document the hilarity and awkwardness that will ensue. Will your target stand there awkwardly or threaten to call the cops? Whatever happens, don’t worry - we’ll be out of there with all our props within 10 minutes and our actors are prepared for any situation and will act accordingly!

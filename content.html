What We Do

We’re a Canadian based company specialize in prank services that feature a large amount of people wrecking havoc at your target’s house. These pranks are not only hilarious and outlandish but they also cause no harm to the target and his or her surrounding environment! You pick the time, date, and location, make a payment, and at the specified time we’ll be there with our props, costumes, and people! Feel free to come watch the prank happen yourself, or if you are unable to make it, we’ll provide you with some sweet sweet documentation created by our resident photographers and videographers at a small fee.

Crime Scene Product Content

The Prank

We’ll set up an extremely realistic crime scene at your target’s house, complete with yellow danger tape, cops pretending to collect evidence around the house, and bystanders crowding around and watching! We’ll dress our employees as cops and bystanders, but if real bystanders end up coming to watch - hey, it’s a free country! Your target will come home to an extremely serious atmosphere and probably panic. If he or she asks any of our employees about the crime scene, they’ll play along accordingly. Ten minutes after your target arrives, we’ll pack everything up and leave without a word. Our videographers will subtly record everything that happens and you’ll have hilarious documentation of the once-in-a-lifetime prank!

Apocalypse Product Content

The Prank

We round up around fifty or so of our best actors and send them off to run over to your target’s house screaming hysterically about an apocalypse. They’ll pound on the doors and walls and yell things like “Please let me in!” and “They’re coming!” It’s then up to your target whether or not they’ll actually let our employees in or not, but rest assure they’re under contract not to damage any of your target’s belongings! They’ll simply keep up the charade by hiding under various furniture pieces. Of course, they’ll have secret cameras attached so we can get hilarious footage of your target panicking. After ten or so minutes is up, our employees will stop what they’re doing and quietly leave - just like that.

Third Wheel Product Content

The Prank

We’ll then fill up the tables with around 15 different couples, and they’ll be acting like they’re on a romantic date.
